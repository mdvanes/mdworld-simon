Because in our new house there is an actual steel reinforced concrete wall between my router and most of my house, there's no WLAN signal anywhere but in my study. That's why, with pain in my heart, I had to say goodbye to my old trusted Linksys WRT54g router and got a Linksys WRT320N dual channel 802.11n router. Because the old adapter I used in snoer didn't support WPA, I also got a new USB adapter, a Linksys WUSB600N. Too bad, off course there's no Linux driver for the adapter. With even more pain in my heart, I reinstalled the factory image with Windows XP :( Well, at least now we've got perfect signal.
