I released version 2 of Insignificant!

Version 2 is a complete redesign of version 1, this time generating the image completely on the client side and as a Drupal module.

Instructions:
- Select a background in the left menu
- Type in the text in the dashed area and drag it around
- Toggle the text balloon, text shadow, the font type, and the font size
- Press "Make insignificant!"
- Right-click the result and select "save image as"

Bitbucket project page: http://bitbucket.org/mdvanes/mdeinsignificant
